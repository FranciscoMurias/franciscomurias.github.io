Superstructures

Homeworld 3

2021 - 2024

Involved from the concept to final on the pipeline and development of the "megalith" superstructures.

Worked on formulating working modular kits, instructed artists on level assembly, scale and detailing, developed set dressing editor tools, involved in solving optimization, lighting, shadow and occlusion culling problems, and developed the shader for the damaged sections of the surface details utilizing "interior mapping" to fake depth with detail.
