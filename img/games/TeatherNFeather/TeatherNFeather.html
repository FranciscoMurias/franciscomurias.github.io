Teather n' Feather

2021

Submitted for GMTK Game Jam 2021.

Developed in 48 hours from the theme "Tied together".

Tether n' Feather is a 3D puzzle platformer game where you must guide two adorable parrots through relaxing and colorful islands.

Tied together, it's your job to make sure that they each safely arrive to their hot air balloon nest. Explore the tether and swing mechanic to glide away through these whimsical levels.
