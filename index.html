About Me

 

Background

I'm Francisco, a passionate professional tech artist who loves to design and develop game experiences and environments. I'm enthusiastic about proceduralism and generative workflows and aspire to bridge the gap between art and programming.

I'm creating games, tools, and interactive art. I like making things that get people to imagine, explore, create and immerse themselves in wonderful fictional worlds.

After earning my BSc in Computer Science from the University of Porto, I'm currently studying MA 3D Games Art at Teesside University in Middlesbrough, UK.
